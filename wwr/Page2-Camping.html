Gardiner, Montana

Nestled at the north entrance of Yellowstone, Gardiner, Montana offers thrilling river rafting on the Yellowstone River. With rapids slicing through scenic canyons and mountains, it's a go-to spot for both heart-pounding whitewater and laid-back scenic floats. Local guides like Flying Pig Adventures and Montana Whitewater make it easy to dive in for an unforgettable splash under the Big Sky.

St. Anthony, Idaho

Absolutely! St. Anthony, Idaho is a hidden gem nestled along the scenic Henry’s Fork of the Snake River—perfect for outdoor lovers and river rats alike. Just 10 miles northeast of Rexburg, it’s your launchpad for tubing, kayaking, and paddleboarding adventures.

Hood River, Oregon

Hood River, Oregon is a Pacific Northwest paradise where adrenaline meets alpine charm. Perched on the Columbia River Gorge and backed by the snowcapped majesty of Mt. Hood, it’s a haven for whitewater rafting, kayaking, and paddleboarding.
